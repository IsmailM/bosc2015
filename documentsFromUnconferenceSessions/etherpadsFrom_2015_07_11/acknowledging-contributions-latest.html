1. Why these features are important for community succcess

Its one of the important factors that motivates members to keep on being an active contributor
  • Helps in self sustaining and increasing visibility within a community
  • Motivating academics by publishing papers using models like BioJS: Professional credit
  • Even a small edit/update should be mentioned: Social credits
    • A profile page for each member where the contributions can be recorded
    • A common update news with the topic "Who's doing what?"
  • Updates of contribution in the documents, release notes, monthly new letters, 



2. tips for making these features happen

  • Actively seek out good/authentic mentors
    • helps in guiding a project during disagreements among members



3. Key challenges for making this happen

  • How to make the community more authentic
    • In a smaller community where people are passionate about certain project and don't care about getting credits until they don't get it (fix the problem before it occurs)
