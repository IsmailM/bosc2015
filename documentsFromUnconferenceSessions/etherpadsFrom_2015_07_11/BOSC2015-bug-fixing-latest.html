Good example: plotly.io, reacts quickly to user requests and bug reports

Quick turnaround time on bug reports and fixes is important.

Of all your users, maybe 5 % interact with you, retain those to turn them into contributors, make the community sustainable.

Communication is key here, people spent time communicating with you, so you should also spend time responding to them.

Make it obvious how to contact you and contribute patches or bug reports. Feedback forms are great, as are issue trackers.

The community's response time to issues and patches is a good measure of the project's viability.

Make it easy and obvious for people contributing to submit the right kind of patches/bug reports. This lowers the effort of contributors to handle patches/issues.
