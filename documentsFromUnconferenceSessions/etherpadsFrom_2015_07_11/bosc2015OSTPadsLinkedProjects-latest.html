High-quality interaction and interoperability with linked/similar projects


+ being a well-defined building block in a universe of well-defined community-developed building blocks

 - Interoperability
    why?
     --more open
     --more usable
     --more useful
     --more accessible
     
     Tips:    
     - communicate with the people in the projects you're ineterestd in being interoperable with
     - develop your interface/API with others with whom you're trying to develop/interact with
     - make the APIs/interfaces documented and kept up-to-date
    - build up the community to help people who don't know they need this to recognized they need this.
     - active to contact to people who really interested in interaction.
     - let the targeted linked communities of users and devs of related software to hold the steering wheel  with you from the beginning!
     
 - Not too big projects trying to solve too many use cases or implement too many layers
    why?
     --complex software is hard to contribute to, maintain, document, finance!, be responsive, be efficiently developable etc
